Safety notes:

1. We strongly recommend that you be fully vaccinated for COVID-19, if you are eligible.
2. Stay home if you do not feel well. If possibly exposed, you must have a negative test within three days of rehearsal. If you miss a rehearsal, please watch the recording via the link sent by email afterwards and added to the calendar date below.
3. We strongly recommend that you wear high-quality masks (for example, N95 or KN95) and observe social distancing.
4. No eating or drinking in the rehearsal room.
